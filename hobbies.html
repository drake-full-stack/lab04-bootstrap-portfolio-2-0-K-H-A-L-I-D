Security Tools I Use:

  • Burp Suite for web application testing

  • Metasploit for penetration testing practice

Our team actually placed 5th nationally in the ISEAGE CDC Hackathon last year, which was pretty incredible! We were even invited to participate in the international competition that happened a few month after.
